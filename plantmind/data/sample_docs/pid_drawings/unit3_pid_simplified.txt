COASTAL REFINERY UNIT-3 — PIPING & INSTRUMENTATION DIAGRAM (P&ID)
==================================================================
Drawing No: PID-U3-001
Rev: 2.0
System: Crude Feed and Distillation Column DC-01 Section

PROCESS FLOW & CONNECTIONS:
---------------------------
1. Crude Oil Tank (TK-001) discharge line connects to suction of Crude Transfer Pump (P-104).
2. Emergency Isolation Valve (V-045) is located on the suction line between TK-001 and P-104.
3. Pump P-104 discharge line flows through Shell & Tube Heat Exchanger (HX-201) shell side.
4. Exchanger HX-201 effluent line feeds directly into Crude Distillation Column (DC-01).
5. Column DC-01 overhead gas line connects to process gas Compressor (C-302).
6. Emergency depressurization line on column DC-01 overhead connects to flare line via Valve (V-046).

INSTRUMENTATION & CONTROLS:
---------------------------
- Control Room Panel (CP-03) handles ESD pushbutton signals.
- Emergency shutdown line trips feed pump P-104, P-105.
- Gas detection system (GDS-07) monitor located in compressor bay near C-302.
- Line CL-015 connects V-045 to P-104.

LEGEND / EQUIPMENT LIST:
-----------------------
- TK-001: Crude Storage Tank
- P-104: Centrifugal Pump (Crude Transfer)
- P-105: Standby Crude Transfer Pump
- HX-201: Feed/Effluent Heat Exchanger
- DC-01: Distillation Column
- C-302: Process Gas Compressor
- V-045: Emergency Isolation Gate Valve
- V-046: Depressurization Valve (to flare)
- CP-03: Control Room Panel
- GDS-07: Gas Detector (LEL sensor)
- CL-015: Crude Transfer Process Line
- AP-2: Muster / Assembly Point Area
